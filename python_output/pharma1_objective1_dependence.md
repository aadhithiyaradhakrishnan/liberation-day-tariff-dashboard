## Interpretation

The U.S. pharmaceutical import base remains highly dependent on a small group of supplier countries. The top 5 suppliers account for 56.5% of imports in 2024 and 56.3% in 2025. Ireland is the largest source in both years, though its share falls from 25.9% to 21.8%. Germany rises to the second position in 2025, increasing its role as a key supplier. HHI falls from 1047 to 942, meaning supplier concentration decreases somewhat, but dependence on major foreign suppliers remains substantial.
